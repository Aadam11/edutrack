The Crisis We're Addressing

In Kano State alone, over 2 million students attend public schools where basic infrastructure is severely lacking. Children sit on bare floors, learn in buildings with leaking roofs, and face daily challenges that no child should endure in their pursuit of education.

Our Mission

Empowering Transparent Educational Reform

EduTrack provides a comprehensive platform that bridges the gap between identifying educational infrastructure problems and implementing sustainable solutions.

By leveraging technology, community engagement, and government collaboration, we ensure that every child's learning environment is safe, conducive, and dignified.

  • Real-time issue reporting and tracking
  • Anonymous teacher protection
  • Government accountability dashboard
  • NGO and donor partnership facilitation
  • Community-driven transparency

How EduTrack Works

Our comprehensive platform addresses every aspect of educational infrastructure monitoring and improvement

Multi-Channel Reporting

Teachers can report issues via web, SMS, or USSD, ensuring accessibility regardless of technological constraints.

Anonymous Protection

Advanced security measures protect teacher identities while ensuring authentic reporting and preventing retaliation.

Real-Time Analytics

Government officials access live dashboards showing infrastructure status, trends, and priority areas requiring immediate attention.

Interactive Mapping

Geographic visualization of issues enables efficient resource allocation and helps identify regional patterns.

Partnership Matching

Connect schools with NGOs, donors, and private sector partners willing to fund infrastructure improvements.

Transparent Tracking

Every report is tracked from submission to resolution, ensuring accountability and measurable progress.
